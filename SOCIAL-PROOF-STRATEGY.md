## What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't over-sell it:**
- Avoid "rave reviews" or hyperbolic language
- No "best session ever" claims
- Keep it factual and understated

❌ **Don't make it the headline:**
- You're an operator first, speaker second
- Let it support your positioning, not become it

❌ **Don't use fake/vague testimonials:**
- If you quote someone, use real names (with permission)
- If you can't quote directly, use aggregate language like you have
- "Overwhelmingly positive" is fine, "everyone loved it" is not

---

## Messaging Principles

✅ **Understated confidence:**
- "Response was overwhelmingly positive"
- NOT "Best-rated session in CCA history"

✅ **Outcome-focused:**
- "Dealers left with practical tools they could implement immediately"
- NOT "Standing ovation" or "Packed room"

✅ **Third-party validation:**
- "Feedback from both attendees and CCA staff"
- NOT "I received amazing feedback"

✅ **Evidence over claims:**
- "These are the same tools that generated that response"
- NOT "These tools will transform your business"
